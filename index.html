About Us

American Legion Selesian Post 599 is committed to honoring the service of veterans and supporting their families. We organize events, provide support, and foster community engagement throughout San Francisco.

Membership

If you have served at least one day of active duty since December 7, 1941, and were honorably discharged or are currently serving honorably, you are eligible to join.
