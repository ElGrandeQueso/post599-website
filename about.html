About Us

Founded in 1921 in San Francisco, American Legion Selesian Post 599 is dedicated to serving veterans, their families, and our wider community.

Our Mission

We empower veterans by simplifying access to benefits, fostering career development through mentorship, and building a strong, connected community. We support veteran entrepreneurs launching and growing businesses, and host networking and educational events to strengthen bonds and opportunities.

What We Do

  • Curate mentor–veteran pairing programs based on field of interest
  • Provide platforms and resources for veteran-owned businesses
  • Host code-and-connect meetups, workshops, and veteran-focused networking events
  • Collaborate with local organizations to expand support and advocacy resources

Why We Matter

By blending innovation, camaraderie, and partnership, Post 599 ensures veterans have the tools, community, and respect they deserve—supporting them through career transitions and beyond.
